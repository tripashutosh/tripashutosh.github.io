About

I am a postdoctoral researcher in theoretical physics, working on holography, QCD phenomenology, and machine-learning methods for forward/inverse problems in holographic models. My current focus includes data-driven reconstruction of holographic potentials and transport.

  • Physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) for inverse problems

  • Consistency constraints: BF bound, causality, and transport bounds
